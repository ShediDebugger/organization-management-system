About OrgMember

A complete platform built for modern organizations

OrgMember brings together member management, event coordination, payment tracking, and reporting in one polished, easy-to-use system.

Designed for associations, clubs, and nonprofit organizations, OrgMember helps teams stay organized while reducing manual work. Manage members, approve events, collect payments, and share updates with the right people.

Our system is built to scale with you — from a single organization to multiple chapters — with secure role-based access and centralized administration.

Powerful Features

Everything you need to run
your organization

From member management to payment processing — OrgMember gives you the tools to focus on what matters most.

Member Management

Add, edit, remove and track members with ease. Assign roles, import in bulk via CSV, and manage member profiles all in one place.

Event Coordination

Create and schedule events, set RSVP deadlines, track attendance and send automatic reminders to your members.

Payment Processing

Track member dues, record manual payments, resend invoices, and monitor payment status — Paid, Due, or Overdue.

Advanced Analytics

Get real-time insights into member growth, event participation, and financial performance with exportable reports.

Role-Based Access

Super Admin, Organization Admin, and Member — each role sees only what they need. Secure and organized by design.

Custom Workflows

Define tasks, set approval processes for events and budgets, assign to members and track progress with deadlines.
